How to Build an AI-Driven Cold Email Icebreaker Machine

Cold outreach isn’t dead—generic outreach is. We show how our clients boost reply rates by 5-10× with personalised intros.

The Bottleneck

Manual research caps you at 10 emails/hour. Automation removes that cap.

What You Need

  • Lead-Generator AI Agent
  • Apify (affiliate) for scraping
  • OpenAI GPT-4o for summaries

Step-by-Step

  1. Scrape 500 leads from Apollo → Google Sheet.
  2. Crawl each site, convert HTML to Markdown, feed into GPT.
  3. Ask GPT: “Write a 2-line opener showing deep research.”

Key Takeaways

  • Specific intros beat “Hey {{FirstName}}” templates.
  • Automation ≠ spam—context still matters.
